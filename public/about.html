About Us

Let's get back to work safely!

For us lives mean everything. In this CovID-19 era we are currently in no one is immune to getting sick and for most of us work is the way we take care of our families and how we put food on the table. And so we need to find a way to not only contribute to society but be safe in doing so for the public for ourselves and our colleagues. We decided back in 2018 that there was a need to make our daily lives safer and more germ resistant hence GermTECH was born. With humble beginnings we sort a better way to combat the now many bacteria, viruses mold and spore populations. But it wasn't until this year 2020 the year of the super covID-19 pandemic did we realise this to be more true than ever.

With that in mind we come at this with a small but dedicated team of talanted people with the same focus TO HELP EACH OTHER GET THRU THIS by tackling it head on. In our own unique way we scaled our business model down to a niche set of products to focus on and do weel at what we do and not get bogged down with trying to do too many things at once we think we've got a good balance and with this small but niche product range we believe we can reach our goal of delivering to our customers the best possible product backed with our suburb service, and ultimately help everyone get back to what they do best.
